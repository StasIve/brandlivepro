The Influencer Trap

The content works. The asset belongs to someone else.

Most "Amazon Live" services run streams from an influencer's account. It looks fine until you ask the right questions: who owns the followers when this ends?

Influencer accounts borrow your audience.

Every follower they earn streaming your products belongs to them. When the collaboration ends, the audience walks. You start over.

Data resets when they stop.

Attribution, click-through, audience demographics — gone the moment they unlist. You can't optimize what you can't see, and you can't retarget what you don't own.

No team. No setup. No time.

You don't want to buy a camera, train a host, write scripts, and learn the Amazon Live Creator app. You want the asset to exist, working, on your account — without you running it.

FAQ

The questions sellers actually ask before signing.

Straight answers — no upsell, no fluff.

How do we know this actually works for our category?
The $150 audit answers this before you commit. We pull your catalog, identify hero ASINs, and benchmark your live-stream-readiness against the categories we've already run. You get a written strategy doc showing the 10-stream content plan, expected Sessions lift, and the specific ASINs we'd anchor on. If the audit shows weak fit, we tell you.
What's the realistic timeline for seeing results?
Week 1: audit, strategy doc, account integration, host vetting. Weeks 2–5: 10 streams broadcast on schedule. Direct attribution shows up within Amazon's 48-hour reporting window. Weeks 6+: the indexing effect compounds. Sessions and Unit Session % continue rising as streams stay live on your listings. Live attribution is fast — but the real lift comes from content compounding over time. We optimize on Sessions, not just last-touch.
Who are your hosts and what's their experience?
Our hosts are vetted on three criteria: on-camera comfort, Amazon Live Creator app proficiency, and category fluency. Every host has run multiple paid streams for brands like yours. During account integration (Week 1), you review and approve the specific host assigned to your product before any broadcast goes live.
What's the ROI compared to spending the same on PPC?
Different math. PPC pays for clicks that stop the moment you stop paying. Amazon Live streams stay indexed on your listings permanently — the same content keeps driving Sessions, follower growth, and conversion long after the $3K is spent. Single streams have produced $9,056 in attributed sales. One client tracked $102K across 6 months from their stream library. The streams become assets that keep producing.
What do I actually need on my end?
Three things: (1) Brand Registry enrollment on your Amazon account. (2) Approved access for our team to stream from your brand account. (3) 30 minutes per month for the strategy review. That's it. Production, scripting, scheduling, hosting, post-stream reporting — all handled.
What happens after the Pilot Month?
You see the data, the lift, and the asset. If it's working, we continue at $3K/month for the next cycle of streams. If it's not, you walk with the streams we've already produced — they stay live on your listings indefinitely. Either way, the content you've built belongs to you.
